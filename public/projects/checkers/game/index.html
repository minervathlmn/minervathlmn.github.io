How to Play?

  • Pieces move diagonally, one square at a time, toward the opponent's edge of the board.
  • If an opponent's piece sits diagonally adjacent with an empty square behind it, you must jump and capture it.
  • Capturing is mandatory: if any jump is available, you must take a jump rather than a plain step.
  • A piece that reaches the far row is crowned a king and can then move diagonally in any direction.
  • A player wins when their opponent has no pieces left or no legal move available.
